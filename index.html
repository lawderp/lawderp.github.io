Increase your chance of getting the house

Gain the upper hand during negotiation, and save money with the power of a cash offer.

Sellers would prefer a cash offer over a traditional financed offer. With a cash offer, the chance of the financing falling through is taken out of the equation, and makes closing on the house drastically easier.

The Dream Home Assurance Program basically acts as a cash backup if something goes wrong and the loan doesn’t close on time (or at all).

Guarantee your home will close

Rent back from us while your loan is finalized. Don’t fret, you can still move in on time.

If you need extra time to secure your loan, and need a place to live, you can still move to your new home with our backup offer guarantee.

With traditional financing, you would need to wait until all of the conditions of the loan were met befroe the loan could be closed. This program allows you to still move in to the house on time.

Protect yourself from financial setbacks

Life happens, we understand. Don’t let an unexpected setback cause you to miss out on your dream.

If your financial situation suffers a temporary setback--job loss, divorce, illness, etc.--not to worry. You can rent the house from us until you resolve the issue and close on the loan.

How it works

Make an all-cash offer in 6 hours.

1) Apply online

Apply for a Novel (all-cash) Offer through our online application, which only takes about 15 minutes. Feel free to use your computer, phone, tablet, or [insert device here]--it all works.

2) Our team will review

Since we’re potentially fronting the cash for your home, we’ll need to review your application to see if you qualify for a Novel Offer. But this is quick—our team will start looking at your file right away, so you’ll be able to make an all-cash offer within 6 hours of submitting.

3) Make an all-cash offer

If you’re approved, we’ll send you a Novel Offer certificate that you can show to sellers as you shop for homes. Sellers love all-cash offers because it’s a sure thing, so you’ll close on your dream home in no time.

4) Get a mortgage

Even though your offer is all-cash, you’ll ultimately be financing your home through a mortgage. So as soon as a seller accepts your Novel Offer, we’ll help you get the loan you need to close. And we'll make sure it's quick—the lenders we work with guarantee Novel customers a turnaround of 7 days (the industry average is 43 days).

5) Move in to your dream home

You’re in! That’s a lot quicker than the traditional mortgage process. And you probably saved thousands (tens of thousands??) of dollars along the way.
