How it works

Make an all-cash offer in 6 hours.

1) Apply online

Apply for LevPlay through our online application, which only takes about 15 minutes. Feel free to use your computer, phone, tablet, or [insert device here]--it all works.

2) Our team will review

Since we’re potentially fronting the cash for your home, we’ll need to review your application to see if you qualify for a LevPlay. But this is quick—our team will start looking at your file right away, so you’ll be able to make an all-cash offer within 6 hours of submitting.

3) Make an all-cash offer

If you’re approved, we’ll send you a LevPlay certificate that you can show to sellers as you shop for homes. Sellers love all-cash offers because it’s a sure thing, so you’ll close on your dream home in no time.

4) Get a mortgage

Even though your offer is all-cash, you’ll ultimately be financing your home through a mortgage. So as soon as a seller accepts your LevPlay, we’ll help you get the loan you need to close. And we'll make sure it's quick—the lenders we work with guarantee LevPlay customers a turnaround of 7 days (the industry average is 43 days).

5) Move in to your dream home

You’re in! That’s a lot quicker than the traditional mortgage process. And you probably saved thousands (tens of thousands??) of dollars along the way.
