Frequently Asked Questions

We know it's complicated. We're here to help.

The basics

What's a LevPlay offer?

A LevPlay offer is effectively equivalent to an all-cash offer: an offer without contingencies for financing.

Why do sellers like LevPlay offers?

Because they're guaranteed, LevPlay offers tend to drive lower purchase prices, are more likely to be accepted in competitive situations, and provide peace of mind for both the buyer and seller.

Applying for a LevPlay offer

How do I qualify to make a LevPlay offer?

You first need to apply to qualify for LevPlay. Your application will then be reviewed and decided on by Movement Mortgage, our exclusive lender partner. If Movement decides that you qualify, you’re then ready to make a LevPlay offer on a house! Note that you do not have to use Movement Mortgage when you ultimately get financing—you’re free to use whichever lender you choose at that point.

Additionally, LevPlay is currently only available for homes with a purchase price of $500,000 or less and in NC, SC, or VA.

Who determines the offer amount for a LevPlay offer?

The amount you qualify for will ultimately be determined by the lender you use to get pre-approved. We partner with certain preferred lenders to help ensure that you have a first-class experience, although you can always use LevPlay with a different lender if you so choose.

Does LevPlay sell my data?

No. The information we collect to qualify you for the program is confidential and will never be sold to other parties.

Shopping with a LevPlay offer

Where does LevPlay operate?

We currently operate in North Carolina, South Carolina, and Virginia. We have plans to expand soon, so stay tuned!

What types of homes qualify for LevPlay?

Only homes that are listed for between $100,000 and $600,000 and in NC, SC, or VA (see above question) are eligible for LevPlay. We plan to expand into other price ranges and geographies soon.

What happens when I find the house I want?

If you find a house that you like and want to submit an offer, you’ll receive a LevPlay “Agreement and Contract Addendum”, which you can then present to the seller.

Pricing

How much does working with LevPlay cost?

$995 if you’re able to secure financing before closing, which the vast majority of our customers have done.

If for some reason you’re unable to secure financing before closing, you can still move in to the home but would have to start paying rent to LevPlay (we would own the house at that point).

How much of a discount will I get when making a LevPlay offer?

The average discount by making a guaranteed offer is 5%. Sellers are often willing to give this discount because a LevPlay offer is guaranteed to close, whereas traditional mortgages are not.

Getting a mortgage

If I use LevPlay, do I still need a mortgage from a lender?

Yes. LevPlay will step in to buy the house if you're unable to secure financing (get a mortgage) before closing, but only if you are working to secure financing (you can rent from us during that time).

To clarify, LevPlay is not a lender. We partner with certain preferred lenders to help ensure you have a first-class experience, although you can always use LevPlay with a different lender if you so choose.

Do I have to use LevPlay's partner lenders?

No. We partner with preferred lenders to help ensure you have a first-class experience, although you can always use LevPlay with a different lender if you so choose.

What if I get a mortgage before my closing date?

Great! You pay a one-time fee of $995 and are all set to move in to your new home at close. This is the most common outcome, and we’ll do our part to make sure this can happen.

What if I don’t have a mortgage by my closing date?

That’s ok, you can still move in to your new home. LevPlay will buy it and you can rent from us for up to 12 months while you work to get a mortgage--see next question for more details.

What happens when LevPlay purchases the home?

If LevPlay is required to purchase the home, we’ll provide all necessary funds in cash to complete the transaction (and take title of the home) on the agreed upon closing date. At this point, you have the option to enter into a 12-month lease with LevPlay to rent the property while you work to secure financing.

If you sign the lease and provide the security deposit requirements, you’re set to move in! Once you successfully secure financing, LevPlay will sell the home to you for the same price we originally paid to purchase the home. At the time of this sale to you, the lease you were in would be terminated.

In the event that you default on the lease, LevPlay will begin marketing the home for sale.

Can I back out from buying the house?

In short, yes. If you want to back out before closing, you can do so free of charge.

If you want to back out after closing on a house (after LevPlay buys the house), you can do so but will be charged fees. See the pricing question for more details.

For agents

I’m a listing agent and received a LevPlay offer. What does this mean?

It means that closing on the home is guaranteed because the financing contingency is removed. If the buyer is unable to secure financing on time, then LevPlay will buy the house at close, no questions asked.
