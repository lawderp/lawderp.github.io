The basics

What's a Novel Offer?

A Novel Offer is equivalent to an all-cash offer, which is an offer without contingencies for financing.

Why do sellers like Novel Offers?

A Novel Offer is preferred because it provides the seller with confidence that the sale is more likely to close. In addition, it allows the buyer to compete in the marketplace for a property that, under a financial contingency, would not allow him or her to compete. It gives peace of mind of guaranteed close and flexibility around terms
